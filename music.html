I make music. Almost daily. Mostly guitar.

Project: Your Fake Noise

Here is the demo album site (work in progress)

This album is a creative response to the changes I've experienced over the 2016-2018 period and the issues of personality, individuality, integrity, betrayal, cult, and reality distortion we face more broadly in societies today.

I continue to write and make tracks for this album. I am also developing an interactive album experience via web technologies and a prototype of writing/recording version control ('MVC' = Music Version Control). You can check out the github page here.
