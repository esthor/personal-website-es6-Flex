BLOG PROJECT

I write to explore topics for myself and show others what I've found.

Project: The unhype Blog

The point of the blog is to express in plain terms different ideas, all related to tech, but spanning across engineering and productivity topics as well as social and historical. It's simply stuff I'm interested in and want to learn more about, so I choose to make a writing project out of it to scope and motivate myself. It is also an exercise in staying away from pitchy, hyperbolic or otherwise hype language. That is why I call the blog, "unhype."

Read about what unhyping is in this blog post.
